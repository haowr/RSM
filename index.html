Oregon-based musician and visual artist Liz Harris has been releasing music under the name Grouper since 2005. Over the years her sound has spanned shades of noise, folk, drone, pop, and ambient, channeled with poignant emotional honesty and a uniquely melancholic mood. Her most recent album, Grid Of Points, pairs skeletal piano with stark vocal harmonies and vulnerable silences, inspired by "the idea that something is missing or cold." Her stateside live appearances are rare but revelatory, obscured impressions of mortality, melody, and the unseen.

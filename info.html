What Is A Web Application?



Your Modern Web.

A web application is the way the modern web user interacts with web content. Users demand a more rich, interactive, and personalized experience from the websites they visit. Really, most websites can be described as web applications. What people mean when using the term 'Web App', however, is an web based application that has the functionality that rivals or even surpasses modern mobile or even desktop applications.

The web is changing. Whether implementing the latest in machine learning algorithms to supercharge stockmarket trading, spotting/diagnosing disease patterns in patients, servicing and shipping to thousands of customers via robust e-commerce solutions, or managing the inventories and employees of fortune 500 companies. The Web based application, is becoming the go-to solution for a multitude of business needs due to its flexibility, availability, familiarity, power, and much more!




What Is A Web Application?

1| Your Customers Are Your Community.

With a web application, your customers are also a community. Create conversation and engagement around product releases, service updates,etc. Making a home for your users creates brand loyalty, and keeps them coming back.

2| Get Real Work Done.

With access to powerful new web frameworks ,JS libraries, and apis, your application has the ability to perform tasks like data analytics, machine learning, facial recognition...Really the sky's the limit!

3| Ensure A Unified Experience.

Ensure updates are pushed to all users at the same time. Making tasks like code testing, new feature engagement, and resource usage a much less stressful experience.

4| No Downloading Necessary.

Users do not download all the content of PWA before using it. Thus, they access its content much faster, directly via a URL. No concerns about space or spending time in the app store means usage rates increase.
